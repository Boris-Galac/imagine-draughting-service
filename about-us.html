Who we are

Formed in 2013 Imagine Draughting Services has grown into an experienced team of CAD Design specialists from the UK and around the world, some of which award winning for their work.


With a mix of varied Degree qualifications and CSWE standard this shows in our service with many clients returning for future projects. We currently have around 25 designers (and growing) on hand mainly based in the UK but also North America, South America, India and Australia so can work to suit any timescale.


We also visit Solidworks launch events around the world to make sure our designers are at the forefront of latest techniques and updates.
